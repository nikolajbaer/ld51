Gnomageddon

The Gnomes have been busily preparing their Grandma's mushroom soup recipe, but those nasty ☠️☠️ skellies from the nearby graveyard really want some. More skellies are arriving Every 10 Seconds!

  • Right Mouse Button / Drag to select Gnomes
  • Left Click to target/move
    • Drop new Gnome houses to recruit more Gnomes Every 10 Seconds
  • Hold Shift and scroll wheel or right mouse to rotate/zoom world

Play Sound (music, grunts, etc)

Made by Kevin, Nikolaj, and Harriet for Ludum Jam 51 (source code for the curious)
